Premium Network for Chef’s

The Culinary sphere has never been more exciting. Thanks to globalization, new ingredients, super foods, novel cooking techniques and cuisines, etc., are taking the world by storm every now and then. The world over, chefs are constantly pushing the envelope and taking food to new heights.

Yes Chef is a unique and an ardent initiative to bring Chefs and F&B professionals around India together to share a dialogue about latest trends in Gastronomy, Bistronomy, kitchen management, and all things food.

It’s a chefs first, community building initiative with focus on career and business growth, skill development and re-education, and networking among the industry professionals. The attendees are in for keynote presentations by F&B influencers, expert panel discussions, live cooking sessions, brand showcase, focused business meetings, casual yet extensive networking, and a riveting hub of ideas promoting open dialogue.

Why Partner

With the Culinary World on rise, YesChef is designed to foster in depth dialogue among the Indian Culinary Faternity.

Don't Miss the opportunity to connect with 150+ Chefs, F&B professionals and Hoteliers across India. Sponsorship initiative for YesChef provides powerful branding opportunities, support with media engagement, consideration for highly-visible program role, one-on-one executive exchanges through speed networking, one to one meetings and extended networking opportunities and much more.

Why Attend

The changing Culinary sphere is taking the world by storm. Chefs are constantly pushing the limits and taking food to a new level. With emerging new technologies as well as methodologies , there is a need for Chefs to be updated about the current trends in the industry.

The 2nd Edition of Yeschef is a place where professionals from the culinary industry will meet to discuss Cooking of tomorrow, with chefs and industry experts ready to share their learnings.
